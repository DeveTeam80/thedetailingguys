What Is Car Floor Lamination?

Car floor lamination involves overlaying your vehicle’s carpet area with a durable, waterproof PVC or PU-based transparent or textured mat, custom-fitted and heat-pressed for seamless edge-to-edge coverage. It adds a protective, wipe-clean layer that preserves the original flooring and enhances interior aesthetics
