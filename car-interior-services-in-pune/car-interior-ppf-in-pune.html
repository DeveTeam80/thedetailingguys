Best Interior Protection Pre-Cut PPF Coating in Pune

Your car’s interior deserves protection too. At Detailing Guys, Undri, Pune, we offer Interior Pre-Cut PPF to shield your dashboard, touchscreen, console, and trim from scratches and wear. Our invisible, high-durability film preserves the factory finish and helps maintain resale value.

What is Interior Pre-Cut PPF?

Interior Pre-Cut PPF is a specially designed transparent polyurethane film, laser-cut to fit your vehicle’s specific interior components. It offers long-lasting protection while maintaining the original look and feel of your interior surfaces.

01 Fingerprints & Smudges

Keeps your screens, consoles, and trims free from unsightly marks and oily fingerprints.

02 Scratches & Scuffs

Defends against accidental scratches from rings, keys, nails, and cleaning cloths.

03 UV & Heat Damage

Reduces the impact of sun exposure, preventing fading, cracking, and discoloration over time

04 Chemical Stains

Protects against damage from harsh cleaners, coffee spills, sanitizers, and cosmetic residues.

05 Wear & Tear

Preserves the new, premium finish of interior surfaces like glossy trims, piano black panels, and touch displays.

06 Easy Maintenance

Makes cleaning effortless dust, smudges, and stains wipe off easily without damaging the original surface.
