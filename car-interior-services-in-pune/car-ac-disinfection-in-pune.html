What is AC Disinfection?

AC disinfection is a specialized service that cleans and sanitizes the internal components of your vehicle’s air conditioning system, including the evaporator, vents, and filters. This treatment removes microbial buildup and improves air quality, ensuring a safer and more comfortable driving environment.

01 Odor Elimination

Destroys foul smells caused by bacteria, mold, and mildew in the AC ducts and vents.

02 Germ & Bacteria Removal

Kills harmful microorganisms that build up inside the AC system over time.

03 Allergy Relief

Reduces allergens, pollen, and airborne particles for a healthier ride.

04 Moisture Control

Helps prevent future fungal growth caused by moisture accumulation in AC coils.

05 Better Cooling Efficiency

A clean system improves airflow and cooling performance.

Our Car AC Disinfection Process

At Detailing Guys, we use industry-recommended tools and solutions to ensure safe and thorough disinfection of your car's AC system:

  • Inspection & Diagnosis: We check for foul smells, poor airflow, and signs of microbial growth.
  • Vent & Duct Cleaning: Specialized tools are used to remove dust, mold, and debris inside air ducts and AC vents.
  • AC Sanitizer Application: We apply a powerful, vehicle-safe antibacterial fog or foam to disinfect the system thoroughly.
  • Evaporator Treatment: A deep cleaning of the evaporator coil eliminates hidden mold and bacteria buildup.
  • Cabin Filter Check: We inspect and suggest replacement if your cabin filter is dirty or clogged.
