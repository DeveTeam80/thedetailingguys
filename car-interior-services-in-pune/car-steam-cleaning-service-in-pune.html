What Is Car Steam Cleaning?

Steam cleaning uses high-temperature vapor to deeply clean and disinfect interior surfaces. It's ideal for upholstery, fabric, carpets, air vents, dashboards, door panels, and other hard-to-reach areas. Steam penetrates into fibers and crevices, lifting stubborn dirt while killing 99.9% of bacteria and viruses without harsh chemicals.

Our Steam Cleaning Process: Purity, Delivered

At Detailing Guys, we ensure a clean, flawless finish through a step-by-step process:

  • Interior Dusting & Prep : We start by removing surface dust and debris to prepare the area for steam cleaning.
  • Steam Sanitization of Cabin : Seats, floor mats, carpets, dashboards, door pads, and center consoles are thoroughly steam-cleaned.
  • Upholstery & Fabric Focus : Special attention is given to seat fabrics, child seats, and high-touch areas for deep sanitization
  • AC Vent Steam Flush : Vents are treated with directed steam to kill bacteria and remove hidden contaminants.
  • Final Wipe & Drying : Surfaces are gently wiped down and aired out for a clean, fresh, and dry finish.
