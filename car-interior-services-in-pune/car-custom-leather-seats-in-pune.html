What Are Custom Leather Seats?

Custom leather seats involve replacing or upgrading your car’s stock fabric or synthetic seat covers with premium leather or leatherette upholstery. Each set is handcrafted to fit your specific car model, offering superior comfort, durability, and a touch of luxury. You can choose from multiple textures, colors, stitching patterns, and design combinations to match your personal taste.

Expert Leather Car Seat Installation in Pune

Whether you're upgrading your brand-new SUV or revamping an older sedan, we offer fully customized leather seat installation using high-precision templates and factory-grade fitting techniques. Our seats look seamless like they came straight from the manufacturer.

Our Leather Seat Customization Process

At Detailing Guys, we ensure a clean, flawless finish through a step-by-step installation:

  • Consultation & Design : Choose your leather type, color scheme, texture, and stitching pattern.
  • Seat Removal & Cleaning : Existing fabric covers are removed, and seats are cleaned and prepared for installation.
  • Precision Fit Upholstery : Custom-cut leather is stitched and applied using industrial machines for a snug, wrinkle-free finish
  • Edge Finishing & Reassembly : All seams and edges are refined before the seats are refitted into your car.
  • Final Quality Check : We inspect every detail alignment, stitching, comfort, and finish to ensure perfection.
