What is Cockpit Deep Cleaning?

Cockpit deep cleaning is an intensive detailing service focused on the front interior zone of your car-the driver and passenger space. Using advanced tools, steam, and pH-neutral cleaners, we remove embedded dust, stains, oils, and allergens from all surfaces without damaging delicate materials like leather, plastic, vinyl, or soft-touch panels.

Expert Cockpit Cleaning Process

At Detailing Guys, our skilled technicians follow a step-by-step interior detailing protocol for flawless results:

  • Interior Vacuuming: We thoroughly vacuum all crevices, seams, and floor areas in the front cabin.
  • Dust-Free Zone: Application is done in a controlled environment to avoid contamination.
  • Surface Cleaning: Using microfiber and safe cleaners, we clean the dashboard, steering wheel, buttons, knobs, and console areas.
  • Steam Sanitization: High-temperature steam loosens dirt and kills bacteria on hard-to-reach areas and air vents..
  • Spot Treatment: We treat stains and oily patches with fabric-safe and material-specific solutions.
