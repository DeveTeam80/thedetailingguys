Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) is a specialized technique for removing minor dents and dings without affecting your vehicle's original paint finish. It's ideal for dents caused by hail, door dings, or minor impacts where the paint has not been chipped or cracked. PDR is a cost-effective and efficient method that preserves the originality and resale value of your car
