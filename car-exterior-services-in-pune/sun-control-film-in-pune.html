What is Sun Control Film?

Sun control film, also known as window tint, is a thin laminate film that can be applied to the interior or exterior surfaces of glass in vehicles. It is designed to reduce the amount of sunlight and heat that enters the vehicle, offering various advantages

01 Heat Reduction

Blocks a significant percentage of solar heat, keeping your vehicle's interior cooler, especially during hot Pune summers.

02 UV Protection

Rejects harmful ultraviolet (UV) rays, protecting your skin and the interior of your vehicle from fading and damage

03 Glare Reduction

Minimizes distracting glare from the sun and headlights, improving driving visibility and safety

04 Enhanced Privacy

Offers increased privacy for you and your passengers, as well as added security for your belongings

05 Improved Aesthetics

Gives your vehicle a sleek and stylish look

06 Increased Safety

In case of an accident, some sun control glass film can help hold shattered glass together, reducing the risk of injury
