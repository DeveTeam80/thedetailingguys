Most Asked Car Service Questions

Polymer coating, often called a paint sealant, is a synthetic liquid polymer that forms a durable, protective layer chemically bonded to your vehicle's paint. Unlike traditional waxes, it acts as a robust barrier against environmental contaminants, harmful UV radiation from the Pune sun, minor scratches, and water spots, keeping your car looking newer for longer.

Polymer coatings offer significantly longer-lasting protection (several months) compared to traditional waxes, which sit on the paint surface. They also provide enhanced gloss, superior hydrophobic properties (repelling water), better UV protection, and make easier maintenance a reality by preventing dirt and grime from sticking. This translates to increased resale value and can be more cost-effective in the long run

While traditional waxes might last a few weeks, a professional polymer coating applied by Detailing Guys in Pune can typically last for several months, often between 6 to 12 months, depending on driving conditions, maintenance, and the specific product used. This provides extended protection against the local climate and pollutants

While optional, paint correction is highly recommended before applying a polymer coating. This process removes existing swirl marks, minor scratches, and imperfections from your paint. A perfectly smooth and decontaminated surface ensures the polymer coating bonds optimally, resulting in maximum gloss, protection, and longevity of the finish

Maintaining your polymer-coated car is much easier! The hydrophobic surface repels water and dirt, simplifying washes. We recommend regular hand washing with pH-neutral car shampoos and avoiding abrasive cleaners.
