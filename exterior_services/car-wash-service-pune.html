Frequently Asked Questions


1. What car wash services do you offer?

Our standard car wash includes an exterior foam wash, underbody cleaning, interior vacuuming, dashboard and trim cleaning, tire and alloy cleaning, and windshield and window cleaning. We aim for a thorough clean every time.

2. Do you do full car detailing, or just a quick wash?

We offer both! You can choose a quick, efficient car wash, or opt for our full car detailing service for a complete rejuvenation of your vehicle's interior and exterior.

3. How do you prevent scratches during the wash?

We take great care to prevent scratches. We use soft microfiber mitts and cloths, pH-balanced shampoos, and premium products. We never use harsh brushes or dirty recycled water, and we start with a pre-wash rinse to loosen dirt safely.

4. Are your car wash methods good for the environment?

Yes, we are committed to being eco-friendly. We use water-saving tools and biodegradable cleaning products to reduce our environmental impact.

5. Can you explain your car wash process?

Our process is very careful. First, we rinse your car to loosen dirt. Then, we apply thick foam and gently scrub with microfiber mitts. We also clean the underbody and tires. Inside, we vacuum and clean the dashboard. Finally, we hand-dry your car with soft cloths and give it a final polish.
