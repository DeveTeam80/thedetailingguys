What is Paint Protection Film (PPF)

Paint Protection Film is a transparent, durable polyurethane film applied to the painted surfaces of your vehicle. This virtually invisible layer provides exceptional protection against

01 Stone Chips and Road Debris

Prevents unsightly chips and damage from flying gravel and road debris.

02 Scratches and Swirl Marks

Resists minor scratches and the swirl marks that can occur during washing.

03 Bug Splatter and Bird Droppings

Protects against the corrosive effects of insect remains and bird droppings

04 Salt and Chemical Damage

Shields your paint from the damaging effects of road salt and environmental chemicals.

05 UV Exposure

Helps prevent paint fading and oxidation caused by prolonged sun exposure.

06 Weather Extremes

Provides a durable barrier against harsh weather conditions like heavy rain, heat, and humidity, keeping your paint looking fresh year-round.

PPF provides exceptional protection against a wide range of damages, including stone chips, road debris, scratches, swirl marks, bug splatter, bird droppings, salt and chemical damage, and UV exposure that can cause paint fading and oxidation

High-quality PPF coatings, like Garware or XPEL, can last 5 to 10 years even in Pune’s hot, dusty, and rainy climate especially with proper care and maintenance.

Yes, many of the PPF options we offer feature advanced self-healing technology. This means that minor scratches and swirl marks on the film can disappear with the application of heat, keeping your vehicle looking pristine.

Installation typically takes 1 to 3 days, depending on the coverage area and vehicle type. Proper surface prep and curing time are crucial for best results.
