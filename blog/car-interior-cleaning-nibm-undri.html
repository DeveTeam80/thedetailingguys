Best Car Interior Cleaning in NIBM-Undri: Process, Prices, Before/After

If your cabin feels dusty, smells musty, or looks tired, a professional car interior cleaning in NIBM-Undri can transform it. At The Detailing Guys, we use a systematic, fabric-safe and leather-safe process to deep clean, sanitize, and protect the interior so it looks and feels fresh again without soaking your seats or leaving residues.

Interior Cleaning Process (Step-by-Step)

  1. Pre-inspection & masking: Check stains, materials (fabric/leather/alcantara), electronics, and sensitive trims. Mask screens and sensors.
  2. High-power vacuuming: Seats, boot, floor mats, under rails, seat pockets, and crevices using soft tools.
  3. Dust & debris removal: Brushing and air-blowing for vents, seams, stitching, and switches.
  4. Fabric shampoo & extraction: pH-balanced cleaners for seats and carpets; controlled moisture with quick extraction to avoid damp smell.
  5. Steam sanitization: Target high-touch areas (steering, gear, door pads), cupholders, tracks, and hard-to-reach zones.
  6. AC vent disinfection: Antimicrobial treatment for ducts to reduce odor and allergens.
  7. Plastic & vinyl dressing: OEM-safe, matte finish—no greasy shine or glare.
  8. Leather cleaning & conditioning (if applicable): Gentle surfactants + UV-guard conditioners to keep leather supple.
  9. Final deodorization & QC: Neutralizes odors; multi-point checklist before delivery.

Before/After - What to Expect

  • Stains: Most food/drink stains lighten substantially; long-set dye stains may need repeat treatments.
  • Odors: Steam + AC disinfection typically remove common smells; persistent smoke odors may require ozone treatment.
  • Dry Time: Seats are touch-dry before delivery; avoid heavy wetting to protect foam and sensors.
